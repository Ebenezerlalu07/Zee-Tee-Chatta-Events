About Us

We are here to help each person to celebrate their special moments with budget price.

Founded in 2019, ZT-Chatta Events was created to provide an affordable event venue in Chattanooga for families and communities looking to celebrate special moments without overspending. After facing the challenge of finding a budget-friendly event space for a family celebration, our founders turned that need into a solution for the Chattanooga area.

Chatta Events is a family-owned event space in Chattanooga, offering a naturally elegant indoor venue inspired by the beauty of the surrounding area. Our venue is ideal for birthday parties, weddings, baby showers, bridal showers, corporate events, and private celebrations.

Our mission is simple: to be the go-to budget friendly event venue in Chattanooga, helping everyone create memorable events in a beautiful, welcoming space.
