About Us

We are here to help each person to celebrate their special moments with budget price.

Founded in 2019, ZT-Chatta Events was created to provide an affordable event venue in Chattanooga for families and communities looking to celebrate special moments without overspending.

Chatta Events is a family-owned event space in Chattanooga, offering a naturally elegant indoor venue inspired by the beauty of the surrounding area.

Our mission is simple: to be the go-to budget friendly event venue in Chattanooga.
